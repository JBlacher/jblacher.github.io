Interactive Projects

Project Summary


Screenshots for an interactive flashed-based training manual for Scotsman Ice Systems. The project was a collaboration with other CG artists using 3d Studio Max, Lightwave3D and Adobe Flash. In addition to coming up with CG elements, I assembled still images into short, timed animations and sync with a voice over in Flash. Basic AS3 was also used to make the project interactive for technicians out in the field that need to assemble and disassemble different sections of the ice machine during repair and maintainence.


Programs: Lightwave3D, Photoshop, Illustrator

Institute of Packaging Professionals


Screenshots from an e-learning training website for the employees of the Institute of Packaging Professionals. The different modules contain the history of packaging and perservation of food, as well as the identification and manufacturing process of modern packaging plastics. These photos highlight a few of the machines and concepts that I animated.


Programs: Lightwave3D, Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator
