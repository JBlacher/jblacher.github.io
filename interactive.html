Interactive Projects

Project Summary


Screenshots for an interactive flashed-based training manual for Scotsman Ice Systems. The project was a collaboration with other CG artist using 3d Studio Max and Adobe Flash. In addition to coming up with CG elements, my job was to assemble the still images into short, timed animations and sync with a voice over in Flash. Basic AS3 was also used to make the project interactive for technicians out in the field that need to assemble and disassemble different sections of the ice machine during repair and maintainence. Institute of Packaging Professionals


This e-learning project was focused on a training website for the employees of the Institute of Packaging Professionals. The different modules contain the history of packaging and perservation of food, as well as the identification and manufacturing process of modern packaging plastics.
