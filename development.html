Total Recon Website


This project was for Total Recon, a small car reconditioning company that wanted to promote its talents to the dealership world. The different areas of repair were show cased by one video animation of a technician doing their job which faded and looped over ten seconds of time on the left, and four static pictures are displayed on the right. The client was looking for a design that was outside of the basic corporate style design.


Programs: Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML/CSS/JQUERY
