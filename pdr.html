PDR

Paintless Dent Removal

My other love in life is my ability to take a car with a million dents and turn it into something that people want, while keeping the automobiles original paint intact. I started paintless dent removal in 2005. PDR involves a series of metal rods of various lengths, that are slid between the interior panels of a car and its bracing and used to push a dent out of a car from the inside out. It is a job that revolves around a high level of patience and an extraordinary amount of hand-eye coordination. There are multiple challenges to overcome.

  • Make the right decision on how to get a tool to the damage and having the right amount of space inside to leverage tool for enough strength to push the metal outward.
  • Finding the right tool for the job. Damage on larger vehicles can be up to 8 feet away.
  • Having a steady hand and a good eye to make a circular pattern.
  • Push down any high spots and straighten even the slightest flicker because in the end everyone knows what a car without damage looks like.
