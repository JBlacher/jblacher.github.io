Cars

Car Obsession

Cars and everything mechanical have been an obsession of mine since the day I had a drivers license. It is no suprise that I wanted to be a mechanical engineer throughout most of high school.

My interest in cars is the challenge of diagnosising and fixing most problems on my own. Repairing a car and web programming use a lot of the same problem solving techniques to achieve a goal. Breaking the engine down into different managable parts and having the knowledge of the job that each particular part performs is the first step. As the different parts are combined together, the second challenge is to know what effect those parts are going to have on each other. The third challenge is to know how all of those parts fit together to make one working engine.
