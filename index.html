About

Welcome To My Portfolio


As a 6 year visual designer living and working in Chicago, IL, my adventure through the world of traditional and digital design has given me experience in the areas of 3d modeling/animation, interactive, print, web, illustration, and most recently UX/UI and front-end development. While I have specific programs that I prefer, I can be given any media tool and find a way to be creative with it to visually communicate with others. I am able to quickly adapt to a company's key softwares. The ability to draw with pencil and paper will always be my greatest asset and the deeper I dive into the digital realm, the more ways I find to use traditional drawing skills.


I love to collaborate with other designers and developers for the mutual exchange in skills as I tend to absorb skills at a rapid rate. My primary focus is visual design but I have gained more than enough all-around skills through personal projects and through startup culture "figure-it-out-type" situations to fill different roles in the front-end development of a project.
