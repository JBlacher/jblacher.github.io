About Me

Welcome To My Portfolio


As a 6 year visual designer living and working in Chicago, IL, my adventure through the world of traditional and digital design has given me experience in the areas of 3d modeling/animation, interactive, print, web, illustration, and most recently UX/UI and front-end development. While I have specific programs that I prefer, I can be given any media tool and find a way to be creative with it to visually communicate with others. The ability to draw with pencil and paper will always be my greatest asset, The deeper I dive into the digital realm, the more I ways I find to use traditional drawing skills


Working within small to midsized groups with other programmers and designers is where I excel. I seek out people with better skill than I have and absorb all the information they can spare until their talent becomes mine.
